# 🎯 Module 4: Bid Optimization & Trend Analysis

This module focuses on evaluating weekly traffic trends, analyzing the business impact of bidding adjustments, and segmenting traffic performance down to device levels (Desktop vs. Mobile) for Maven Fuzzy Factory.

---

## 📌 Business Problem & Context
* **Context:** Monitor weekly session volume variations and test the elasticity of paid search traffic in response to bid changes.
* **Goal:** Optimize ad spend and bid strategies based on channel performance and device-level conversion trends.
